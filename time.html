Time Series Analysis

It is important to understand the trends over time when it comes to traffic accidents. This section visualizes the total number of accidents over the course of four years as well as the frequency distributions by day and by time of day.

The line chart below shows the total number of accidents per month from January 2015 to December 2018.


The bar chart pictured below shows the frequency distribution of accidents that occured on each day of the week.


This bar chart, seen below, shows the frequency distribution of accidents that occured per hour of the day in 24-hour time, with 0 representing 12am and 23 representing 11pm. There are two additional layers that can be applied to this chart via the drop-down menu: Serious Injuries and Fatalities.

Serious Injuries shows the number of serious bodily injuries that resulted from the accidents that occured on each hour of the day, with darker shades indicating more injuries. Fatalities shows the number of deaths that resulted from the accidents that occured on each hour of the day.
