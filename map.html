Map of Accident Locations

It is first and foremost important to understand where the accidents are occuring within the City of Denver. The map below shows the locations of all accidents recorded between 2015 and 2018. The colors indicate each of the 78 neighborhoods in Denver in which the accidents took place. The bar chart shows the total number of accidents for each neighborhood and can be used to filter individual neighborhoods on the map.
