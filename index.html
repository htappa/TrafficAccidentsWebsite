Introduction

Motor vehicle accidents are one of the leading causes of death in the United States, and a major concern for most US cities. This website is designed to visualize motor vehicle accident data from Denver, CO. The dataset includes records of crashes that were reported to the Denver Police Department that occurred within the City and County of Denver during the previous four calendar years (2015-2018).

The two tables on the right detail the number of serious injuries per accident and the number of fatalities per accident in Denver between 2015 and 2018. The factors and details surrounding these accidents will be further analyzed within this website, which is divided into three distinct categories:

  • Map of the accident locations
  • The causes of the accidents
  • The number of crashes per month and frequency distributions of crashes by day and by time of day
